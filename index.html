About Us

WHY MOTION PHYSICS?

MOTION PHYSICS Private Centre has been transforming students into confident problem-solvers since 2023. Located in the heart of Phulpur, we specialize in turning abstract science and mathematics concepts into intuitive understanding.

Our pedagogy bridges the gap between rote learning and deep comprehension — helping students not just pass exams, but develop a learning mindset.

  • Qualified and experienced teachers
  • Weekly mock tests & detailed feedback
  • Doubt-clearing sessions every Saturday
  • Printed notes + digital resources provided
  • 100% syllabus coverage for JSC, SSC
